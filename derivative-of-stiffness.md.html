Need derivative of stiffness to compute model derivatives. Assume linear elements, quadratic strain measure, StVK model. $$ \begin{align} \delta^2 \FF &= \delta^2 D_s = \mathbf{0} \\ \delta^2 \EE &= \delta \FF^T \delta \FF \\ \delta^2 \PP &= \FF (2 \mu \delta^2 \EE + \lambda \tr(\delta^2 \EE) \II) \\ &= 2 \mu \FF \delta \FF^T \delta \FF + \lambda \tr(\delta \FF^T \delta \FF) \II \\ &= 2 \mu \FF \delta \FF^T \delta \FF + \lambda (\delta \FF : \delta \FF) \II \end{align} $$ Have to verify by numerical test.
