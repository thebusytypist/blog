The most straightforward way to couple fluid and rigid is the Gauss-Seidel style iteration. From implementation point of view, the dofs and simulation code are separated (except for some motion states and pressure/torque exchange), thus good modularity can be achieved. It gives convincing result but may suffer from drifting or convergence issue. ![Figure [incompressible-weak-coupling]: Weak coupling](images/weak-coupling.mp4 width="250px")
